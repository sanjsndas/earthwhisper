30 Detailed Points About Cookies

This section offers a comprehensive explanation of various aspects related to cookie usage on our website to help you understand how cookies affect your browsing and data privacy.

  1. What Are Cookies?

    Cookies are small files that websites place on your device to store information about your preferences and interactions. They help improve user experience by remembering login details, language preferences, and site navigation behavior, making your visits more efficient and personalized.

  2. Types of Cookies We Use

    Our website uses several types of cookies including strictly necessary cookies, performance analytics cookies, preference cookies, and marketing cookies. Each serves a unique function, from enabling website access to measuring site traffic and personalizing advertising.

  3. Strictly Necessary Cookies

    These cookies are essential for basic website functions and security. Without them, the site cannot function properly, and these include session identifiers and load balancing cookies that maintain the website's stability and user session continuity.

  4. Performance and Analytics Cookies

    We utilize performance cookies to gather anonymized insights into how users interact with our site, allowing us to identify popular content, navigation issues, and opportunities to improve overall user satisfaction and performance.

  5. Preference Cookies

    Preference cookies capture choices you make on our site, such as language or region, and help maintain these settings across visits. This ensures that your experience feels consistent and tailored to your needs.

  6. Marketing Cookies and Targeted Advertising

    Marketing cookies track your browsing activity across websites to deliver more relevant advertising. They help identify trends and measure promotional effectiveness while ensuring ads are aligned with your interests.

  7. Third-Party Cookies

    Sometimes, third-party services such as social media or analytics providers place cookies on our site. These cookies are governed by the third parties' privacy policies. We strive to work only with trusted partners who adhere to data protection standards.

  8. How We Use Cookies

    Cookies enable us to enhance site usability, remember your preferences, analyze traffic, and provide personalized marketing. They are vital to delivering a seamless and customized user experience that respects your privacy choices.

  9. Managing Cookie Preferences

    You can manage your cookie settings anytime through the consent banner on our site or your web browser settings. This includes accepting or rejecting non-essential cookies to control the information shared during browsing.

  10. Disabling Cookies

    Disabling cookies in your browser settings may restrict certain website features or personalized services, and may require you to manually re-enter preferences on subsequent visits.

  11. Cookie Consent and Legal Compliance

    EarthWhisper complies with GDPR and other privacy regulations by obtaining user consent for non-essential cookies. We offer transparent explanations and options to accept or decline cookies before placing them.

  12. The Purpose of Our Cookie Consent Banner
