Privacy Policy

Learn how EarthWhisper collects, uses, and protects your data to ensure your privacy while providing expert irrigation consulting and rich content.

Privacy Policy Overview

At EarthWhisper ("we", "our", or "us"), your privacy is of utmost importance. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We are committed to transparency and compliance with all relevant data protection laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable regulations.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about our users, including:

  • Personal Data You Provide: When you subscribe to our newsletter, fill out feedback forms, or contact us, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you voluntarily provide.
  • Technical and Usage Data: We collect technical information automatically, including your IP address, browser type, device identifiers, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and time spent on the site through cookies and analytics services.
  • Location Data: Depending on your device settings, we might collect approximate geographical location data to offer relevant content and comply with legal obligations.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect data that enhances your experience and supports our analytics and advertising partners.

Legal Bases for Processing Your Data

Under applicable laws, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we rely on one or more of the following legal bases to process your personal data:

  • Consent: Where you have given clear consent for us to process your data for a specific purpose, such as subscribing to newsletters.
  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill obligations under any agreements between you and EarthWhisper.
  • Legitimate Interests: For our legitimate interests in operating and improving our website and services, provided such interests are balanced against your rights.
  • Compliance with Legal Obligations: Where required for compliance with applicable laws, regulations, or government requests.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing, operating, and maintaining our website, blog, and consulting services.
  • Responding to your inquiries, requests, and feedback efficiently and effectively.
  • Personalizing your experience and presenting tailored content, including relevant irrigation consulting information and tips.
  • Sending marketing communications, newsletters, and promotional offers where you have consented.
  • Monitoring and analyzing usage trends to optimize the website and content delivery.
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues and security incidents.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Recognize you on subsequent visits to our website, remembering your preferences and settings.
  • Understand and analyze the usage of our website, improving navigation and user experience.
  • Provide personalized advertising when applicable and allowed.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note, disabling cookies may impact functionality and your user experience.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as hosting providers, analytics companies, or email service providers. These parties are contractually bound to keep your data secure and confidential.
  • Law enforcement agencies or regulators if required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • Affiliates or business partners in case of reorganization, merger, or acquisition.

International Data Transfers

Because we operate globally, your personal data might be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. We ensure that such transfers are protected through enforceable data protection agreements following international standards like the EU-US Privacy Shield Framework or Standard Contractual Clauses.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, to comply with legal requirements, enforce agreements, resolve disputes, and protect our legal rights. After this period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights and Choices

You have important rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access and Portability: Obtain confirmation whether we process your personal data and receive a copy in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccuracies or completion of incomplete data.
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data where permitted by law.
  • Restriction: Limit the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Objection: Object to the use of your data in direct marketing or when processing is based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw Consent: When processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting legality of previous processing.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@earthwhisper.com. We will respond within legally mandated timeframes.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent. If you believe your child has provided personal data to us, please contact us immediately so we can delete that information.

User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for keeping their login credentials confidential and notifying EarthWhisper immediately of any unauthorized access or personal data breaches.

Security Measures

We employ industry-standard and appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or destruction. This includes encryption, firewall protection, and secure server infrastructure.

However, no internet transmission or electronic storage can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will promptly notify affected users and relevant authorities in accordance with applicable laws. We maintain procedures to mitigate breaches and prevent recurrence.

International Users
